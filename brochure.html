Program Overview

The STEAM Adventure Camp is designed for middle school students seeking hands-on, project-based learning. Over 8 days, students work in structured teams to design and build a functioning community — complete with electronic houses, programmable robot cars, bridges, towers, and an economic system. The program culminates in an expo where each team presents the community they built.

Program Focus

This program emphasizes both hard and soft skills essential for development in future education and in the workforce.

Core Projects

Students work in pairs to build one electronic house and one programmable car. Three pairs then form a community team of 6.

IoT Smart House

Build a smart house with the ESP32 IoT kit. Wire up sensors, LEDs, and automation rules. Customize as a hospital, fire station, or community center.

Week 1: Days 1 - 4

Robot Car

Build and program a robot car with the ELEGOO Smart Car kit. Control motors, add lights and sounds. Program it as an ambulance, bus, or plow truck.

Week 2: Days 5 - 8

Community Building

Teams of 6 come together to build a full community around their electronic projects. Each community includes:

3
Smart houses & robot cars
B

T

Students also create an economic system, community structure and values, and infrastructure for their town.
