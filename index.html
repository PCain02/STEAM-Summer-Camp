What We'll Explore

Science is all about asking questions and finding answers. This summer, we'll learn the scientific method, run real experiments on plant growth, and discover how electricity powers our world — from batteries to the power grid.

Scientific Method

Learn to think like a scientist — ask questions, form hypotheses, design experiments, collect data, and draw conclusions.

Technology

Build real electronics projects with Arduino, sensors, and motors — then program them to serve your community.

Engineering

Design structures, solve real problems, and build the infrastructure your community needs.

Community Infrastructure

Every community needs structures — bridges to connect, towers to communicate, and buildings to live and work in. Your team of 6 will design and build the infrastructure that holds your town together using real engineering principles.

Design and build a bridge out of spaghetti and glue that can hold the most weight. Learn about trusses, tension, and compression.

Art & Language Arts

Design, draw, write, and present — the liberal arts skills that bring every STEAM project to life.

What We'll Create

The "A" in STEAM is about more than painting. It's liberal arts — visual design, architecture, writing, and communication. You'll draft blueprints, discover patterns in nature, write professional reports, and present your work to an audience. These skills tie every other subject together.

Floor Plans & Maps

Design floor plans for your community's buildings and draw a full map of your town using graph paper, rulers, and colored pencils.

Design & Present
